Moonwalk

Zero-friction REST APIs for Lua.

For normal use, the only module you'll need to require is moonwalk . The readme covers most of what you need to know to create an API, but you may want to look at the docs for moonwalk and moonwalk.config .

You may also want to use drive to help serve files, or proto for simple OOP-style inheritance, or take advantage of the text processing utilities in util .

To add support for a new host environment, see connection.abstract . For ideas on creating your own stand-alone server, take a look at the server.socket and server.luanode source .
